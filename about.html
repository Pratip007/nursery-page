About Ritwika Nursery

The Premier Plant Nursery in Contai Town - Your Trusted Partner in Horticultural Excellence

Our Story

Ritwika Nursery, under the leadership of Proprietor Bhabatosh Giri, has established itself as the premier plant nursery in Contai town. We specialize in growing and supplying a comprehensive range of plants, including fruit trees, flowering plants, show plants, and roadside plants. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to destination for all your horticultural needs in the region.
